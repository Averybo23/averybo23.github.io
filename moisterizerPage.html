Moisterizer

The moisturizer, this step is crucial even if you have acne and oily skin. For the longest time, I thought that because I have oily skin I can skip moisturizer, but that causes your pores to produce even more oil. Even if you have acne chances are you use lots of exfoliants and drying agents so keeping your skin barrier healthy is important. This is not to say that all skin types can use the same moisturizer and if you have oily skin you will probably want a thin cream for the night and a good sunscreen that's non-comedogenic. My favorite is La Roche Possay's Tolerane moisture barrier repair, and for my sunscreen, all of the facial sunscreens from Elta MD.
